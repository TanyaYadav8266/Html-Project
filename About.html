About Our Shop

Welcome to Blooms And Potion


At B&P, we believe in the power of flowers to brighten up any occasion and bring smiles to people's faces. We are passionate about providing our customers with the highest quality flowers, exceptional customer service, and unique floral arrangements that truly capture the essence of beauty and elegance.

Our History:

B&P was founded in 2010 by Tanya Yadav, who had a deep love for flowers and a vision to create a special place where people could find exquisite blooms for every occasion. With a background in floral design and a keen eye for detail, Tanya started the shop with a small collection of flowers and a commitment to exceptional craftsmanship. Over the years, B&P has grown into a thriving floral destination, serving the local community and beyond. We have built a strong reputation for our attention to detail, personalized service, and a wide variety of flowers sourced from trusted growers and suppliers. We take pride in being a part of our customers' most important moments, from birthdays and weddings to anniversaries and celebrations of all kinds.

Our Mission:

At B&P, our mission is to create memorable floral experiences that touch the hearts of our customers. We believe that flowers have the power to convey emotions, celebrate milestones, and add a touch of natural beauty to any setting. Whether you're looking for a romantic bouquet, a stunning centerpiece, or a simple gesture to brighten someone's day, our dedicated team is here to help you find the perfect arrangement.

Our Services:

  • Custom Floral Designs: Our talented team of florists is skilled in creating unique and personalized floral arrangements tailored to your preferences and occasion. From traditional to contemporary styles, we have a wide range of options to suit every taste.
  • Special Occasions: We specialize in providing flowers for weddings, anniversaries, birthdays, corporate events, and other special occasions. Our team works closely with you to understand your vision and bring it to life with breathtaking floral creations.
  • Delivery Services: We offer reliable and timely delivery services, ensuring that your flowers arrive fresh and vibrant to your desired location. Whether it's a surprise gift or a bulk order for an event, we take care of the logistics so you can focus on enjoying the beauty of our flowers.
  • Floral Consultation: If you need guidance in choosing the right flowers or want advice on floral arrangements, our knowledgeable staff is always available for consultation. We can help you select the perfect blooms that match your style, budget, and occasion.
